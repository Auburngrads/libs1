classify_emotion classifies the emotion (e.g. anger, disgust, fear, joy, sadness, surprise) of a set of texts.
classify_polarity classifies the polarity (e.g. positive or negative) of a set of texts.
create_matrix creates a document-term matrix.
emotions a dataset containing words categorized into six emotion categories.
subjectivity a dataset containing a list of positive and negative subjective words.
